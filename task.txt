PROJECT REQUIREMENT ANALYSIS: EcoClean LtdCase Study: Eco-Friendly Product Line Development1.
 PROJECT OBJECTIVET :to define the requirements for a new line of sustainable cleaning products by analyzing market trends and consumer preferences to ensure a competitive advantage.
2. DATA GATHERING PLAN (Research Phase)To identify the key features, the analyst will use:
Market Trend Analysis: Researching "Green Chemistry" and circular packaging (refillable/biodegradable).
Competitor Benchmarking: Comparing Eco Clean against existing brands (e.g., price points, certifications like Eco-Cert).
Consumer Surveys: Identifying if customers prioritize "Scent," "Price," or "Chemical-free" formulas.
3. PRELIMINARY REQUIREMENTSA. Functional Requirements (Features)Formula: The product must use plant-based surfactants instead of petroleum-based ones.
Packaging: The bottles must be made from 100% Post-Consumer Recycled (PCR) plastic.
Trace ability: A QR code on the label must link the user to a web page showing the source of ingredients.
B. Non-Functional Requirements (Quality/Constraints)Regulatory Compliance: Must meet Rwanda Standards Board (RSB) environmental safety standards.
Performance: The product must match or exceed the cleaning power of traditional chemical cleaners.
Sustainability: 95% of ingredients must be biodegradable within 28 days.
4. PROJECT SCOPE 
In-Scope: Researching ingredients, eco-packaging design, and target audience identification.
Out-of-Scope: The actual manufacturing process and the nationwide distribution logistics.
